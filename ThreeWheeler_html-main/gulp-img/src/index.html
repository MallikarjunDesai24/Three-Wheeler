More than One Million

Happy Customers Across the Globe

TVS Motor Company, the flagship company of TVS Group, is one of the largest three-wheeler manufacturers in India with an annual production capacity of more than 2,40,000 three-wheelers. The company is also one of the leading three-wheeler exporters from India distributing to over 43 countries.

The journey of TVS King started with a small step 14 years ago and has now become a legacy with over 1 million happy customers across the globe.

TVS - a brand trusted by millions, has 100+ years of experience in Automotive Mobility. TVS Motor Company is involved in indigenous design, development, and manufacturing of wide segment of IC & ELECTRIC two wheelers viz. motorcycles, scooters, mopeds, and portfolio of three wheelers which caters to passenger mobility and cargo segment under its brand.Show more

TVS Motor Company launched its first of its Kind 3-wheeler in 2008. It was India's First 200CC “Electric Start” 2 Stroke Three-Wheeler named as “TVS King “which is indigenously designed and developed in India at its R&D to ensure a best in class “Auto” to the Indian Roads by its unmatched combination of Power, Comfort, Style & Mileage.

In the year 2012, TVS King Deluxe was launched with its Fuel Efficient 200 CC Air-cooled Engine "Mileage Maharaja" along with many first in its segment features such as Easy to operate reverse gear, Car like Dashboard, Powerful twin headlamps, Chassis mounted bumper & Dual-Tone driver & passenger seats, transforming this "Auto" into a "Car on Three-Wheels"

TVS King Duramax comes with the latest cutting-edge technology - A 225 CC Liquid Cooled Engine, an innovation in the segment. This technology provides a brilliant combination of Power, Performance and Speed and for sure retaining the high fuel economy of TVS King. TVS King Duramax is low on maintenance with periodic service frequency of 10000km and longer warranty period of 50000kms/365 days.

Both Deluxe and Duramax are available in Petrol, LPG and CNG variants.

TVS King Kargo introduced in 2021, is the cargo three-wheeler. Due to higher speed & riding comfort of King Kargo you can carry the goods quickly to your destination and make more trips per day putting extra money in your pocket. TVS King Kargo comes with adequate power & pickup for city roads, best in class stability and handling. First in its segment feature " i- touch Silent Start " along with Bi-Fuel Gas (LPG / CNG) with Petrol mode provides endless & very smooth drive. King Kargo can be modified for different business needs such as ice-cream on wheels, Egg carrier, Fire Tender Van, Dumper Van, Food on Wheels, Milk can carrier, Vegetable Van, Water can carrier.

Over the14 years, TVS King has established itself as a strong brand due to its performance, mileage, durability, and quality in India and established its dominance in International Market such as LATAM, Africa, South Asia, and South East Asia.Show less
